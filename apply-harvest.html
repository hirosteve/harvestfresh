How it works

All vendors or resellers that will like to purchase from HarvestFresh are referred to as Vendors

1. A vendor will fill the registration to join the waitlist on HarvestFresh

2. HarvestFresh Team will perform a confirmation of the Vendor’s interest and confirm their registration on the platform

3. Confirmed Vendors will be able to place their orders from the platform by contacting the sales department. Note that these orders are fulfilled on a First Come First Serve basis.

4. HarvestFresh will notify vendor of receipt of order and will notify vendor of current farm price for the order received.

5. After successful payment confirmation, Orders are delivered to vendor’s preferred locations.

Delivery Location

All orders are currently delivered to the southwestern parts of Nigeria. Lagos | Ogun | Osun | Oyo Other Destinations outside south west Nigeria will have to be picked up directly from the farm by vendors.
